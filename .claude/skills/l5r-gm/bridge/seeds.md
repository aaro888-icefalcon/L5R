# Seed Sources — Rokugan / L5R 5E   (hook: seeds)

> **Operative digest:** Refresh the campaign seed deck to **30–40** candidate beats **every bookkeeping**, drawn
> near the PC from canon, live world state, and honest generator rolls. Seeds are candidates, not scripted
> events — the dice still decide if and how one fires.

- deck size: 35            # 30–40 candidate beats kept ready for scenes / Turning Points / Random Events
- refresh: each bookkeeping
- sources (drawn near the PC, weighted toward what is live and proximate):
  - **campaign `setting-canon.md`** entries near the PC (places, NPCs, relics, open mysteries)
  - **the bridge `setting-canon.md`** (Rokugan ground truth) for clan/region color in reach
  - **live world state** — open Threads & vows, weighted Characters, active Clocks, faction
    standings, the giri/ninjō pressure, any conflict carried out of a scene
  - **random rolls for novelty** on:
    `generators/family_name.json`, `generators/given_name.json`, `generators/npc_role.json`,
    `generators/location.json`  (re-skinned & placed in the current region/court)
  - **L5R content hooks** — a clan agenda, a Fortune's festival, an ancestor's debt, an omen,
    a rival's technique, a duty come due
- consumed when a seed is invoked by a scene, Turning Point, or Random Event (then refreshed).
- The main AI populates the deck inline at bookkeeping; the optional **mythic-scout** agent can
  offload the harvest (`mythic-gm/references/scout.md`).
