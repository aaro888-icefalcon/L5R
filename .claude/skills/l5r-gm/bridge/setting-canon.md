# Setting Canon — Rokugan (the Emerald Empire)   (companion ground truth; overrides recollection)

> RPG-level ground truth for **every** L5R campaign. A *campaign's* own discovered facts live in
> `campaigns/<slug>/setting-canon.md` and take precedence over this for that campaign.
> **Deep lore is in `.claude/skills/l5r-gm/references/setting/`** — read the relevant file before
> inventing (intro-rokugan · emerald-empire · celestial-realms · shadowlands · courts-of-stone ·
> fields-of-victory · path-of-waves · writ-of-wilds).

- **Premise / tone:** A feudal-fantasy Japan of seven Great Clans and many Minor Clans under a
  divine **Emperor**, descendant of the Sun. The soul of play is **giri (duty) vs ninjō (desire)**,
  and **honor / face** is the true currency. Restrained, formal, melancholy-beautiful; the
  supernatural (kami, ancestors, ghosts, the Shadowlands Taint) is real and near.
- **The Celestial Order:** everyone has a place — Emperor → Great Clans → Minor Clans → ronin →
  commoners (heimin) → the unclean (hinin/eta). Station governs who may speak, sit, or strike.
- **The Great Clans (agenda — register):**
  **Crab** (Hida/Hiruma/Kaiu/Kuni/Yasuki) — hold the Wall against the Shadowlands; blunt, dutiful.
  **Crane** (Doji/Kakita/Daidoji/Asahina) — art, courts, peerless duelists; proud and lethal.
  **Dragon** (Togashi/Mirumoto/Kitsuki/Agasha) — mountain mystics; cryptic, introspective.
  **Lion** (Akodo/Matsu/Kitsu/Ikoma) — the Emperor's army; martial honor and ancestors.
  **Phoenix** (Isawa/Shiba/Asako) — masters of the kami and the void; scholarly, self-righteous.
  **Scorpion** (Bayushi/Shosuro/Soshi/Yogo) — the Emperor's underhand; secrets, blackmail, deniability.
  **Unicorn** (Shinjo/Moto/Iuchi/Utaku/Ide) — returned gaijin-touched riders; forthright, foreign.
  **Minor Clans** (Moshi/Toritaka/Yoritomo/Tsuruchi/Suzume/…) — scrappy, slighted, specialized.
- **The supernatural:** Five Rings (Air/Earth/Fire/Water/Void) underlie all; **shugenja** beseech
  the **kami**; **ancestors** watch and obligate; the **Shadowlands Taint** corrupts body and soul;
  ghosts, oni, and spirits of the realms (Tengoku, Yomi, Jigoku, Sakkaku, Ningen-dō) press on the world.
- **Bushidō (the code):** Courage, Compassion, Courtesy, Duty (sincerity), Honor, Righteousness,
  Self-Control. Violating it openly costs **honor**; upholding it can cost **everything else**.
- **Hard content lines:** maho (blood magic) · mature themes · gaijin content are **OFF** by default
  and only enter play if the *player* opts in aloud.
