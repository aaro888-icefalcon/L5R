## Places
- **Toshi Ranbo** — the contested town on the Lion–Crane border; the flashpoint of their war (Akodo Arasou died here, 1123, to Doji Hotaru's arrow). Akihiko's Legion is posted to the front.
- **Toritaka Province (the Valley of the Spirits)** — Akihiko's haunted Falcon homeland, north by the Shinomen; his family's seat. Far from the front.
- **Otosan Uchi** — the Imperial capital; the seat of his uncle Tadanobu's power and the Throne whose edict the Legion enforces.

## Factions & clans in play (name — agenda — disposition to the PC) · see facts.py
- **The Imperial Legions** — the Emperor's own multi-clan army; Akihiko's employer. Mostly Lion-fielded; commanded by Seppun and the clans' best. Enforce Imperial edicts (incl. caps on clan troops) and settle inter-clan war. Disposition: he is theirs, watched, a junior officer with a famous patron.
- **Lion Clan** — Right Hand of the Emperor; largest army in Rokugan; vengeful at Toshi Ranbo. Resent being policed by the Throne's Legion. (`facts.py clan lion`)
- **Crane Clan** — Left Hand of the Emperor; smaller, exquisite army; masters of court & the duel; ancestral enemies of the Lion. (`facts.py clan crane`)
- **Scorpion Clan** — bar their doors to Akihiko (his adversity *Scorn of the Scorpion*); ever-present in any court/war as the Emperor's underhand.
- **Falcon (Toritaka)** — Akihiko's home minor clan; spirit-hunters allied to the Crab; little political weight. He carries their spirit-sight into a mundane war.

## Named NPCs (name — role — want — secret)
- **Seppun Tadanobu** — Akihiko's illustrious maternal uncle, reclaimed into the Seppun; an Imperial servant of real standing; the patron who placed him in the Legions. **Want:** UNKNOWN — roll it; his favor is a leash with a price. **Disposition:** proprietary, demanding, watchful.
- **Toritaka Michio (b. Seppun Michio)** — maternal grandfather; the talented black sheep who left the Seppun to marry into the Toritaka. *(deceased; the family stain.)*
- **Toritaka Sumiko** — mother; half-Seppun, carries the openly-known Imperial line; retired Falcon healer-warden.
- **Toritaka Akiro** — younger brother, apprentice healer-shugenja; the dearest bond.
- **Moshi Hizashi** — father; Centipede sun-priest-physician; how Akihiko got his Moshi Sun Sentinel training.

## The known heritage (campaign-defining)
Akihiko's Seppun (Imperial) blood is **openly known**, and **complicated**: high enough to court favor or envy, low enough that he is always the *black sheep's grandson*, riding on his uncle's prestige with something to prove. Mechanically: **Blessed Lineage** (Status 50+ won't kill him first). It is a lever others reach for — leverage, not just honor.

## Timeline / recent events
- **1123 (default 5E era):** Lion Champion Akodo Arasou slain at Toshi Ranbo by Crane Champion Doji Hotaru's arrow; the Lion–Crane war escalates. The Throne moves to contain it — the edict Akihiko's Legion carries.
- Akihiko, reset to the start of his service, has just been commissioned **chūi** in an Imperial Legion company through his uncle's sponsorship, and posted to the front.
