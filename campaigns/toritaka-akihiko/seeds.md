## Deck

### The morning's body — Gennai silenced (the cold open, Scene 13)
- **The dead spymaster** — Akihiko wakes (or is woken) to find his one live lead murdered in the warded cellar overnight. Inspect the scene & the method: how did a knife reach a maimed prisoner inside a Champion's keep? A Falcon-issue blade, a poison, a strangling-cord, a "suicide" too neat?
- **Who held the watch?** — the cellar guards: bribed, blind, asleep, dead, or one of them gone missing? The first thread to pull — and a man who may be the next to be silenced.
- **An inside hand** — the enemy walks these halls. Read the household: a new face, a servant who shouldn't have keys, a retainer who flinched. (Ties to the Lion-lie's "rot is among us.")
- **They moved fast** — was it ruthless prudence (erase an exposed asset), or a *reaction* to the charge Chiyo gave in a cleared room last night? If the latter, someone listened to a sealed door.
- **Tell Chiyo, or work it first?** — her investigation's first casualty is hers before he's started. How she takes the news (and how he frames it) — and charge #3 in motion: does he watch her face when he tells her?
- **Matsu's stake** — the Emerald Magistrate's live Scorpion prisoner is now a murdered witness on Falcon ground. Imperial jurisdiction, her wrath, her help — and her awkward Lion blood as he probes a Lion-benefiting plot.

### The Lion-lie investigation (Chiyo's charge #2)
- **Ikoma Naoki** — the velvet broker: summon him, receive him, or go to him? Read whether the protectorate is genuine Lion ambition or a script someone handed him. A courtier duel of courtesy & sentiment.
- **The manufactured threat** — chase the "evidence" that built the lie: the raids that didn't happen, the moved border-stones, the misread patrol. Walk the marches and find the seams (Survival/Sentiment/Tactics).
- **Ujiyasu, read not broken** — the frightened karō is the door the lie walked through. Earn the old man's account without snapping him: who first whispered "Lion," and when.
- **The Scorpion craft / Lion gain knot** — the central puzzle: why would a Scorpion hand hand the prize to the Lion? A false-flag, an Imperial trap, a faction within a clan, or a third player using both.
- **Would the Throne sanction it?** — the Imperial question: a letter to grandmother **Seppun Yuki**, or to the Emerald Champion's office; does Imperial law even *permit* a Minor Clan swallowed whole? A lever, if pulled.
- **The deep-power shape under the politics** — Akihiko's private dread: a Falcon dissolved into the Lion stops warding the Shinomen. Does the hand behind the lie *know* that — is this the dark's move wearing a courtier's face?
- **The Scorpion in the cellar's masters** — with Gennai dead, the cell that silenced him is still here; pull on the courier-network, the safehouse, the next listener.

### The Mirror's price — a way that doesn't consume the anchor (Chiyo's charge #1)
- **Yoshi & the binding-lore** — sit with the head Yotogi priest and the Mirror: what do the founder's writings actually say about the anchor? Is "spend yourself" the only reading, or doctrine hardened by 1200 years of grief?
- **A second anchor** — could the burden be shared, rotated, or borne by more than one willing soul? The Yotogi priesthood as a chain of anchors rather than a single martyr?
- **The founder's loophole** — the First Yotogi bound, not died; the Keeper lasted 1200 years. Was there a *technique*, not just sacrifice? Seek the lost method (Theology/Meditation/old texts).
- **His father's heresy** — Moshi Hizashi treats invocations as natural philosophy; a letter or visit — does the radical sun-priest see a mechanism where the orthodox see only doom?
- **The Mark as the key, revisited** — his wound is the leash to the power. Could the binding be *aimed* through the Mark without pouring his soul into the bowl?
- **Ask the Mirror itself** — probe the relic (Theology/Meditation); it shows him something true and costly. (Now in Yoshi's keeping, under the priests' eyes.)

### What's wrong with Chiyo (charge #3 — the hidden fear)
- **Watch her** — the fear she walled off is real and specific and not the Lion-lie. Catch the tell: when does the iron slip? What name, place, or hour makes her flinch?
- **The thing she's too frightened to see** — she licensed him to name it. Earn the door open a second time (Sentiment/Sixth Sense), or surface it sideways through someone close to her.
- **Her isolation** — friends who'd report her, soldiers loyal to the chair, an old man who sold the clan a rope. The new Champion trusts exactly one man, and that's its own danger to her.
- **The weight of the seat** — a peer who broke Lions' noses now carries a drowning Minor Clan, a war of fog, and a relic that eats its wielder. Does she crack, harden, or lean?

### The deep power / the spirit-dark (looming, quiet for now)
- **The wounded power regroups** — hurt for the first time in 1200 years, fixed on Akihiko; it reaches again, harder, when his beacon-blunting fades. (No new sign the night of Sc.12 — but it gathers.)
- **A new sign of the harvest** — another emptied hamlet, a fouled warding-shrine, a cold portal tearing near the marches; the Falcon's shrines may no longer be safe.
- **The taken souls** — Suzu and the freed boy, soul-shocked at Kyūden Toritaka; do they stir, speak a word from the deep, or fade? The Mirror is their only full cure.
- **The Mark aches** — near a breach or Taint; the double-edge gives Akihiko a faint lead on where the power stirs (GM-facilitated).

### Home, kin & the new court
- **Akiro** — the brother-healer tending Suzu & the freed boy; the dearest bond, a place to be just a man for an hour.
- **Seppun Yuki (grandmother)** — the Imperial matriarch: summon, test, or shield; her weight could answer the "would the Throne sanction it" question — or complicate it.
- **The Scattered Corps** — a distant comrade's call (Jōji near at hand; Eniko, Genzō, Bara, Hiroyuki, Saburō, Kasumi afar); the new duty isolates him from them.
- **Jōji's blunt counsel** — his closest comrade-Warden names the hard truth Akihiko is avoiding about the charge he just accepted.
- **His abandoned post** — the tri-border march (Hare–Falcon–Crab) now held by another while he serves the Champion; a loose squad, a worried lieutenant, a border that misses its yōjimbō.
- **The Crab question** — ancient allies conspicuously bypassed by the Lion deal; reach out to Hiruma Genzō / the Crab border-lords — would they have warded the Falcon if asked?
