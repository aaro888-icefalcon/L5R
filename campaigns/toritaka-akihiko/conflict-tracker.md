## Kuchinashi, the Mouthless Gaki  [Otherworldly, Tainted]
- Rings: Air 3 · Earth 2 · Fire 1 · Water 2 · Void 3 · Vigilance 3 · Focus 4
- Skills: Martial 2 · Scholar 2 · Social 1
- **Insubstantial Form:** physical damage resisted (Physical resistance 5). **Sacred weapons ignore all its resistances.** Sun/Sacred Fire hits full and is especially potent.
- **Soul-Rend** (Scheme/Attack, range 0–2, supernatural): on a hit, inflicts heavy **strife** (claws the soul) instead of wounds; ignores physical resistance. A target who **unmasks** under its assault gets soul-hooks set (→ hollowing).
- **Anchored to the Soul-Gate:** at 0 Endurance it withdraws into the gate and reforms next round. True banishment = re-consecrate the shrine.
- **Recoils from the Sun (Repulse):** Sacred/sun-fire vs it is especially effective; while a Sacred flame burns *at the shrine*, it cannot drag new souls through.
