## Dead / departed Characters
- **Toritaka Genkō — "the Keeper of the Barrow"** — the bound grave-fire spirit (an ancient Falcon watchman set by the First Yotogi) who guarded the Dawn Mirror for 1200 years. **Mercy-killed by Akihiko with Lady Sun's fire (Scene 5)** before the deep power's rot could turn him — the bargain kept, against Akihiko's Softheartedness; he passed whole.
- **Kuchinashi, the Mouthless** — the gaki that made Hibari Mura's sun-shrine a soul-gate and harvested ~40 people. **Banished by Akihiko's Sacred sun-fire (Scene 1)** when he sealed the gate; the taken souls were dragged north into the deep Shinomen (taken, not destroyed — see open Threads).
- **Toritaka Haru** — a young Falcon ashigaru of Akihiko's escort; **killed instantly by a Scorpion throwing-blade opening the sunken-lane ambush (Scene 9).** Carried home for honorable burial — the cost of the road.
- **Lord Toritaka Hatsue, "the Yotogi"** — Falcon Clan Champion / daimyō; spirit-seer who spent the last two years of her sight staring down the deep power so the scorned Warden she chose could grow into facing it. **Died at Kyūden Toritaka (Scene 11)** — having held herself alive past her time to receive the Dawn Mirror, name Akihiko *"the other inheritance"* (the clan's ward, not its seat), and confess the Mirror's price — that it consumes its living anchor — with her last breath. Akihiko's lord, hard mentor, secret-sharer, and the one who chose him. *Her daughter Chiyo holds the seat; Akihiko carries the ward.*

## Closed adventures (title — outcome — Themes)
- **Arc 1: The First Ward** — Akihiko recovered *Asahi no Kagami,* the Dawn Mirror, from the First Yotogi's barrow; it confirmed his Imperial blood and settled its binding-duty onto him. Strong capstone; the long campaign (cure the mark · free the souls · bind the deep power) opens from here.
